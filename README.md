# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
Analyzed e-commerce sales data using Power BI to evaluate sales performance, profitability, and customer demand patterns across products, regions, and time. Built an interactive dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  

---

## Dataset
- Source: Flipkart Sales Dataset  
- Fields: Orders, Products, Categories, Sales, Profit, State, Date, Payment Mode  

---

## Key Insights
- Electronics generates the highest sales, making it the top revenue-driving category  
- Clothing contributes the highest profit, highlighting strong margins  
- Sales show seasonality with peaks in early months and decline mid-year  
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, and Telangana lead in sales performance  
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most used payment method (~45%), followed by UPI  

---

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing on high-performing products like Printers and Sarees  
- Improve profit margins in high-sales categories through pricing optimization  
- Launch promotions during low-sales periods to stabilize revenue  
- Expand marketing in underperforming regions to capture growth opportunities  
- Encourage digital payments (UPI, Cards) to reduce dependency on COD  

---

## Business Impact
- Improved decision-making through clear visibility of sales and profit trends  
- Identified opportunities to increase revenue and profitability  
- Enhanced regional and product-level strategy planning  
- Supported operational efficiency through payment insights
